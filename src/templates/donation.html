How it works

We try to be fully transparent to you and seek to reduce our costs, so we work on a pledge fulfillment system. This means that what you are making is a pledge. Usually, in a week, we will send a statement to your email with a link for you to fulfill all of the unfulfilled pledges. We have found this is most convenient for those who donate while reducing the change to costs related to pull-back donations. Once we receive your fulfilled pledge, we notify the receipient.
